Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TalentHub.

Who can join TalentHub?

TalentHub is open to all current students of Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU) who are interested in art, design, music, photography, and other creative pursuits.

How do I upload my artwork?

Once you are a registered member, you can access the Upload Center from your dashboard. Simply fill in the details of your work and upload your high-resolution files.

Is there a membership fee?

No, TalentHub is a free platform provided by the university to support student creativity and collaboration.
